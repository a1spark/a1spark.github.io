Macro Photography Terms

Magnification Ratio: The ratio between the size of the subject and the size it appears on the sensor.

Depth of Field: The distance between the nearest and farthest objects in a scene that appear acceptably sharp.

Focus Stacking: A technique combining multiple images at different focus distances to increase depth of field.
